My story

Kemal Tekin first fell in love with the art of programming when he was young. Growing up, he was more fascinated with how video games were made, than actually playing them. He was still at school when he took his first foray into the world of programming and hasn’t looked back since.


By the time he reached Grade 4, he had already programmed his first game. While he was studying for his Bachelor’s in IT and Master’s in Business Education, he gained a lot of experience by completing freelance work for clients from all over the world.
